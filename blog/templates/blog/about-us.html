About Us

    Monika Romo

    I am a Mexican American who was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois. When I was in 8th grade I was first introduced to computer science from a STEM program I was apart of. The teacher we had introduced us to HTML and CSS by having us use Mozilla Thimble. Over the years I have become more passionate about Computer Science and plan on majoring in Cyber Security.

    Tinuola Adebukola

    I was born in Nigeria and came to America as an immigrant at the age of 7 and was raised in Chicago, Illinois ever since. My intesrest in computer science was excited when i joined my school's Girls Who Code Program and from there I learned about the summer immersion internship. Our final project was to solve a problem we felt strongly about and use a piece of code to construct the solution. I'm proud of our outcome and plan on minoring in computer science and majoring in pre-medicine. I'm thankful to have had this opportunity.

    Elizabeth Loma

    I, also, am a proud Mexican American, born and raised in Chicago. I recently became interested in computer science thanks to Girls Who Code. If it weren't for my counselor who told me about the program, I would have never come come across this amazing opportunity. Thanks to GWC, I learned how to use HTML and Python, among others. Computer science has changed my life in many aspects and I definitely encourage others, especially women, to look into learning more about computer science.
